How to Make Brownies with only three Steps

Ingredients Measurment
oil 3/4 cup
Powdered or Granulated Sugar 3/4 cup
Vanilla Extract 1&1/2 tsp
eggs 3
All purpose flour 3/4 cups
Coca Powder 1/3 cups
Baking Poweder 1/2 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp

Instructions and Steps

 
        Step 1: Mix the oil, the sugar, vanilla extract, and eggs in a separate bowl

        Step 2: Mix the flour, coco powder, baking powder and salt in another bowl

        Step 3: Mix both together and add coco powder according to your taste

        Step 4: ENJOY!
